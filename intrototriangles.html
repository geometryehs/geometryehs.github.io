Intro to Triangles

Classifying Triangles

A triangle is a shape with three sides and three angles. We can classify a triangle by either its sides, its angles, or both. Let's start by classifying a triangle by sides.

  • An Equilateral Triangle is a triangle with three congruent sides.
  • An Isosceles Triangle is a triangle with two congruent sides.
  • A Scalene Triangle is a triangle with no congruent sides.

Remember that congruent sides means that they are equal in length. To classify a triangle by its angles, we need to find the largest of its three angles first.

  • An Obtuse Triangle is a triangle whose largest angle is greater than 90 degrees.
  • A Right Triangle is a triangle whose largest angle is exactly 90 degrees.
  • An Acute Triangle is a triangle whose largest angle is less than 90 degrees.
  • An Equiangular Triangle is a triangle that has three angles that have the same degree measurement.
